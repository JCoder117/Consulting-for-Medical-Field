<h2>Description</h2>

Assisted in facilitating trainings and plan of actions for Marayane Medical Laboratory located in South Africa. While incorporating health and compliance standards utilized in the US, EU and Botswana. These trainings would consist of best practices, terminology, quizzes. Also the curation of a risk assessment matrix to target potential weak points of infrastructures. Pertaining to potential threat actors and vulnerabilities this was complete through information reconnaissance (liason). Ultimately producing a risk assessment, IRP, recommendations and training materials through the policies and procedures from technology and healthcare compliance standards.
